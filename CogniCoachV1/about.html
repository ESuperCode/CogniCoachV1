We are a company that was built upon a problem: When practicing by yourself, how do you make workouts to improve on certain things? You can't, because you can't be the best coach and the best athlete at the same time. This is how CongiCoach was born, to do the coaching part, so you can focus on becoming the best athlete you can be, so you can train on your true rigid schedule with your real coach but also your flexible schedule with CogniCoach.
